How it works

1) Choose a candidate

You sponsor a relative or motivated young person (18+) who wants to build a professional pathway in property.

2) Fund the partner activation

The sponsorship is €1,000 and covers training plus onboarding and activation into the PanAfric ecosystem.

3) They become certified

After completing modules and onboarding, they can operate as a Partner under our standards and compliance rules.

What the €1,000 covers

Training — €250

  • Access to Partner Academy modules + quizzes
  • Practical scripts, templates, and deal workflow
  • Certification after completion

Onboarding + Activation — €750

  • Partner profile setup + verification steps
  • Tools orientation: listings, leads, and client onboarding
  • Launch support: first listings + first client pipeline
Important: This is an empowerment program. It is not a donation, and we do not promise income or results. Performance depends on the partner’s effort and market conditions.
