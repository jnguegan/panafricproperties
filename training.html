Professional Standards. Trust. Compliance.

PanAfric Properties operates a trusted real estate marketplace serving diaspora and international buyers. To protect clients, maintain platform credibility, and ensure professional conduct, all franchise partners must complete our internal training and certification program before onboarding.

Our Training & Certification Program ensures that every partner:

Understands Real Estate Fundamentals

Learn the basics of property markets, valuation, and transaction processes.

Follows Due Diligence Standards

Understand verification procedures, fraud prevention, and documentation checks.

Operates Ethically

Adhere to transparency, professionalism, and client protection principles.

Complies With Platform Governance

Follow reporting, compliance, and marketplace rules.

How the Partner Academy Works

1) Apply & Get Accepted

Submit your franchise application. Approved candidates receive access to the Training Platform.

2) Log In to the Training Platform

Access our internal online learning system using your personal login credentials.

3) Study the Training Modules

Read structured modules covering real estate basics, due diligence, ethics, and platform processes.

4) Complete Short Tests

Each module includes a short assessment to verify understanding.

5) Earn Your Certification

Once all modules and tests are passed, you receive Partner Certification.
