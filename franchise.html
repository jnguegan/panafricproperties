From Graduate to Real Estate Entrepreneur

We empower young Africans — graduates, non-graduates, and unemployed professionals — to launch their own self-employed real estate businesses in their home cities under our franchise model. PanAfric Properties provides training, in-house certification, and access to diaspora buyers through our trusted marketplace platform.

Important Clarification

PanAfric Properties is not offering salaried employment or traditional jobs. This program is a franchise-based entrepreneurship opportunity. Successful applicants operate as independent, self-employed business owners under the PanAfric Properties brand and trust framework.

Who This Is For

Graduates & Young Professionals

If you have completed university or vocational training and are seeking a practical, income-generating business opportunity in your home city.

Non-Graduates With Ambition

You do not need a formal degree to succeed. We provide training, structure, and certification to help you operate professionally.

Unemployed Professionals

If you are currently unemployed or underemployed and want to become self-employed in a growing sector with real demand.

Diaspora Returnees

If you are returning to Africa and want to build a credible, structured business using an established brand and diaspora buyer pipeline.

What We Provide

Training & Onboarding

Structured onboarding covering real estate basics, client handling, due diligence procedures, platform processes, and ethics.

In-House Certification

All partners complete certification to operate under the PanAfric Properties brand and trust framework.

Brand & Marketplace Access

Use our brand, platform, and marketplace infrastructure to list verified opportunities and reach diaspora buyers worldwide.

Buyer Leads

Receive qualified enquiries from diaspora and international buyers routed through our marketplace.

How the Franchise Model Works

Territory-Based

You operate in a defined city or territory as a PanAfric Properties partner. Exclusive or semi-exclusive rights may apply depending on market size.

Commission-Based Earnings

Earn commissions on successful property transactions and client referrals completed through the platform.

Independent but Structured

You run your own self-employed business while operating under our standards, trust framework, and compliance processes.

Growth Path

Top-performing partners may grow into multi-city operators, territory managers, or regional franchise leads.

Trust, Ethics & Compliance

Verification Standards

All listings must pass basic legal, ownership, and documentation checks before being published on the platform.

Code of Conduct

All partners operate under a professional code of ethics covering transparency, client protection, and fair dealing.

Ongoing Monitoring

We monitor partner performance and compliance to maintain marketplace trust and diaspora confidence.

Platform Governance

Partners operate within our marketplace governance framework to protect buyers, sellers, and the PanAfric Properties brand.
